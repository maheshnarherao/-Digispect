Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Digispect Technologies ("we," "our," "us," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number, postal address
  • Business Information: Company name, industry, business size
  • Service Inquiries: Information about services you're interested in
  • Form Data: Any information you voluntarily submit through our contact forms
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Browser Data: Browser type, version, IP address, referral URL
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, mobile device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, clicks, scroll behavior
  • Cookies: Session cookies, tracking cookies for analytics
  • Local Storage: Data stored locally for website functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • To process your requests and respond to inquiries
  • To send promotional emails and newsletters (with your consent)
  • To analyze website usage and improve user experience
  • To prevent fraud and ensure website security
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To personalize content and recommendations
  • To conduct market research and surveys
  • To send system updates and support messages

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist in our operations (analytics, hosting, payment processing)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your information
  • Aggregated Data: Non-personally identifiable data for research and analytics

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Session Cookies: Maintain your login session and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Track website performance and user behavior
  • Marketing Cookies: Personalize ads and track campaign effectiveness
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Secure password hashing for stored credentials
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Secure data deletion procedures
  • Compliance with industry security standards

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Objection: Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@digispect.com

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Retention periods vary based on the type of information:

  • Contact Information: Retained for service provision and up to 3 years after last contact
  • Analytics Data: Retained for 12-24 months
  • Marketing Preferences: Retained until you opt-out
  • Legal/Compliance Data: Retained as required by law

Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before submitting your information. We use the following services:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics for website usage tracking
  • Hosting: Third-party server infrastructure
  • Email Service: Email marketing and communication platforms
  • Payment Processing: Secure payment gateway providers

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information and terminate the child's account immediately. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your country of residence, which may have different data protection laws. By using our website, you consent to such transfers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including standard contractual clauses.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the website after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
